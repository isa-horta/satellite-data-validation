**Statistical validation tests: gauges, CHIRPS and IMERG.**

- Script used for statistical validation tests of daily rainfall data from gauges, CHIRPS and IMERG.

- Outputs were saved in .csv format and plotted in maps using QGIS.

- Area of Study: The Paraiba's Valley and North Coast of the Sao Paulo state, Brazil.

- Time series: from 2000-06-01 to 2018-12-31.

- Results are discussed in the following publications: 

1) Horta, I.T.L.G., 2025. Impact of seasonality on rain-triggered landslide hazard modeling (Doutorado em Ciências da Engenharia Ambiental). Universidade de São Paulo, São Carlos. https://doi.org/10.11606/T.18.2025.tde-29102025-161958


2) HORTA, I. T. L. G.; BORTOLOZO, L. A. P.; Quevedo, R. P.; MAUAD, F. F.; TECH, A. R. B.. DAILYRAINFALL DATA VALIDATION: IMERG, CHIRPS, AND GAUGES.. In: ANAIS DO XX SIMPóSIO BRASILEIRO DE SENSORIAMENTO REMOTO In: XX Simpósio Brasileiro de Sensoriamento Remoto, 2023, Florianópolis.
Anais do XX Simpósio Brasileiro de Sensoriamento Remoto. 2023, v.20, p.970 -973
